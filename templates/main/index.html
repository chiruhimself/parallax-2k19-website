What's Parallax?

Parallax is a three days intercollegiate festival organised by “Self Financing Courses Students”. Parallax aims at bringing out the spark and skill in students through its exclusive events. The events at Parallax include varied competitions, Cultural events And professional shows which will be conducted within the college premises.

Be a Globetrotter and hunt down the mysteries of the Histories. It's time to compete and rule Parallax on 7th, 8th and 9th January 2019.
